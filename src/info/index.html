Welcome to the Books Finder.

Through the search bar we can type in a category of books we want.

For example: Fantasy, Historical Fiction, Horror, Humor, Stories in Rhyme, Baby Books, Fashion, Film, Graphic Design, Music.... and more.

Then, you can, by clicking on the book cover, view its description.

This application contacts APIs offered by Openlibrary.



(The categories with multiple names not available, ex.: 'Thriller Horror')
